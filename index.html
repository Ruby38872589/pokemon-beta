About The Protagonist

Riko, a girl from Paldea, has a mysterious pendant.
Roy, a boy from Kanto, has a mysterious Pokéball.
A new adventure begins for Riko and Roy in the vast world of Pokémon!What encounters and destinies await them?This is a story about "discovering" something important through adventure.

Introduction To Main Items

Important items that will appear in the plot

Brave Onion :
The spaceship aboard Rico, Roy and the other members of the Shoryu Volt Attack Team.
There are two types: air flight and sea navigation
Configuration on board: steering room, conference room, engine room, power distribution room, medical room, kitchen,Observation room, air bag, flying wing deck (battlefield), deck, dormitory
Rico's Pendant :
The pendant that Riko received from her grandmother turns out to be the mysterious Pokémon Terrapagos!
Terrapagos :
It slept in the form of a pendant for a long time, but after reuniting with the Six Hero Pokémon, it returned to its original Pokémon form.
(Riko's grandmother : She is an adventurer who travels all over the world.She teaches Riko and the others about Lucius and the Six Hero Pokémon.)
Ancient Poké Ball :
A Poké Ball said to have belonged to the ancient adventurer Lucius.It contained the Six Hero Pokémon that once traveled with Lucius.
Lucius :
A real Pokémon trainer who was once known as the "Ancient Adventurer."
He is said to have traveled with the Six Hero Pokémon - Black Rayquaza, Oliva, Galarian Fire, Lapras, Basagiri, and Entei - as well as Terrapagos, and reached the paradise "Rakua"...!?
